Instructions

Overview

Your goal is to advance as far as possible within a series. A series consists of three fails. A fail happens when you throw your disk off the board, run your disk into an obstacle, or you do not get your disk to come to rest within the target area in three or less throws. Each throw is taken from the resting point of the last throw. If you get the disc in the target, you will advance to another round with more obstacles. If you are able to get your disc in the target area within less than the three throws, you will be awarded another fail in your series. You will receive one point for advancing each level.

How to Throw a Disk

You can throw a disc by clicking on the disk, dragging the mouse back to create power and direction, and then releasing the mouse button to send the disk sliding across the board.
